Italo
House
-fx-background-image: url('file:resources/pictures/house_music_icon.png')
There was a big backlash to everything Disco in America in 1980. In Europe, this didn't happen, so Disco naturally progressed over there to what it had been hinting at for several years: a stripped down, mechanical version of itself dubbed Italo Disco for reasons unknown as very few of the artists were actual Italians.
